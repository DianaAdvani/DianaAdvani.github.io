Indians Short Film

Indians is a short film I am currently developing which explores immigrant culture, race relations, and the power of minorities banding together. This story is based on my father's experience immigrating to the US from India in the 80s. He moved to South Dakota and befriended a group of Native Americans, Yellow Thunder Camp, who were occupying land and lobbying the federal government to succeed this land back to the Sioux tribe.

Character Design

Suresh is displayed as both a human and a monkey. Suresh is based of my father. Pictures of my father can be seen in a collage in the background of the picture.

Manny is displayed as both a human and a coyote. This character is inspired by Native Americans my father met in South Dakota. The group he met occupied land in the Black Hills and called themselves Yellow Thundercamp. The collage in the background are pictures of Yellow Thundercamp.
