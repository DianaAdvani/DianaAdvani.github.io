Indians Short Film

Indians is a short film I am currently developing which explores immigrant culture, race relations, and the power of minorities banding together. This story is based on my father's experience immigrating to the US from India in the 80s. He moved to South Dakota and befriended a group of Native Americans, Yellow Thunder Camp, who were occupying land and lobbying the federal government to succeed this land back to the Sioux tribe.
